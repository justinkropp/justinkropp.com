Practice

Justin is a digital product designer based in Chattanooga, TN. He currently works for Mozilla as a Senior UX Designer. His expertise and interests sit at the intersection of design, research, and strategy. He is particularly interested in how traditional principles of graphic design can be leveraged to create exceptional digital experiences without the pretense.

Work

Justin has led interactive and identity projects for clients in education, non-profit, architecture, and cultural sectors. Previous client engagements have included Coca-Cola, UPS, The International Olympic Committee, Johns Hopkins, Tufts University, Alys Beach, Rosemary Beach, ActionWhere, ServisFirt Bank, GlobalPost, and many others...

Education

He studied graphic design at the Maryland Institute College of Art, graduating in 2010 with an MFA in graphic design.
