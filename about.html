Overview

I’m a designer — exploring my discipline through technology, typography, and semiotic theory (that last part’s a stretch). I currently serve as a user experience designer at Automattic, the makers of WordPress. Previously, I was the design lead at Readmill, which was acquired by Dropbox.

I earned an MFA in Graphic Design from Maryland Institute College of Art, where my graduate thesis explored the role semiotics plays in our evolving visual culture.

To supplement my practice, I speak, write, and teach and I’ve been fortunate enough to do all three in a professional capacity.

Ethos

I believe in the power of design to affect positive change in the world. Pursuing that change, no matter how big or small, is a worthy goal. I believe that creative work should be inpsiring and rewarding — to both the creator and the consumer.
